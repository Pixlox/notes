#japanese/hai-4/unit-3 

### First 10 days
---
Dates are slightly complicated. The first 10 dates are completely different and new, however they use a familiar same numerical kanji, then {日|にち} format. On top of this, the 14th, 20th and 24th day are in a slightly different format. 

- **1st day:** {一日|いちにち}
- **2nd day:** {二日|ふつか} 
- **3rd day:** {三日|みっか} 
- **4th day:** {四日|よっか} 
- **5th day:** {五日|いつか} 
- **6th day:** {六日|むいか} 
- **7th day:** {七日|なのか} 
- **8th day:** {八日|ようか} 
- **9th day:** {九日|ここのか}
- **10th day:** {十日|とおか}
- **14th day:** {十四日|じゅうよっか}
- **20th day:** {二十日|はつか}
- **24th day:** {二十四日|にじゅうよっか}


### The rest of the calendar
---
The rest are pretty straightforward; stating the number, then {日|にち}. 

For example, the 11th is {十一日|じゅういちにち}.


### Question structure
---

> [!tip] When asking when something is, you can use いつ.

***Asking for just date:***
**Q:** きょうは{何|なん}{日|にち}ですか
What is today's date?

**Q:** きょうは (date) です。
Today is (date).


***Asking for full date:***
**Q:** きょうは{何|なん}{月|がつ}{何|なん}{日|にち}ですか
What is today's [[Months|month]] and date?

**Q:** きょうは ([[Months|month]]) (date) です。
Today is ([[Months|month]]), (date).


***Asking for full date, with day:***
**Q:** きょうは{何|なん}{月|がつ}{何|なん}{日|にち}{何曜日|なんようび}ですか
What is today's [[Months|month]], date, and [[Days of the week|day]]?

**Q:** きょうは ([[Months|month]]) (date) ([[Days of the week|day]]) です。
Today is ([[Months|month]]), (date), and it is ([[Days of the week|day]]).
