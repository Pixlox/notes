#japanese/obento-y10 

### Asking 
---
**ごちゅもん　は　何ですか？**
What is your order?

**何　に　しますか？**
What have you decided on?

- Talking about decisions - に　します。

### Giving order
---
**(Thing)　を　ください。**
(Thing) please

(Thing)　に　します。
I'll decide on (Thing).

### Asking for how many (Quantity)
---
**Q:** いくつですか？
(How many?)

**A:** 二つください。
**A:** 二つです。
(I'll take two.)

- Use counters for asking for items or referencing them.
- No need to use つ past 十. Just say the number.
- eg. Donuts をふたつください.
