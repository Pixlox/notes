#japanese/hai-4/unit-4

### New kanji
---
When expressing how much we like something, we say すき. Now we have a new kanji.
- 好, pronounced す.
- So now, we write 好き.


### Like levels
---
- **Regular liking:** (Thing) が{好|す}きです。 
- **Loving:** (Thing) が{大好|だいす}きです。 
- **Favourite:** (Thing) が一ばん{好|す}きです。
